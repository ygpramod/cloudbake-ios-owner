# CloudBake Owner App Wiki

This wiki explains the CloudBake owner app from a product and bakery-business point of view.

Use it to understand what the product is for, how handmade cake workflows are represented, and what
the app currently supports. Engineering source truth still lives in `docs/adr/`, `docs/rfc/slices/`,
tests, and code.

## Source Of Truth

Repo-local `wiki/` is the source of truth. The GitHub Wiki is the publication target after changes
are merged to `main`.

For each future slice, update wiki source when the slice changes product behavior, business rules,
owner workflows, terminology, or durable product truth. If no wiki update is needed, record that
decision in the PR or final handoff.
